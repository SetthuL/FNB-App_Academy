PANDA

The panda is a bear species endemic to China, known for its distinctive black and white fur. It primarily feeds on bamboo and is considered a national treasure in China.

Pandas are known for their gentle temperament and playful behavior, making them a favorite among zoo visitors.

Where will you find it?

The panda exhibit is located in the Asian section of the zoo, near the tiger enclosure. You can observe them from the viewing platform that offers a great view of their habitat.

Don't miss the panda feeding session at 1 PM daily, where you can see these adorable creatures munching on their favorite bamboo!
