LION

The lion is a species in the family Felidae and a member of the genus Panthera. It is the second-largest living cat after the tiger.

They are known for their majestic manes and powerful roars, which can be heard from miles away.

Where will you find it?

The lion exhibit is located in the African section of the zoo, near the elephant enclosure.

Come and watch the lions during their feeding time at 2 PM daily!
