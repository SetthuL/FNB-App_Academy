PIZZA SHOP

Craving something cheesy and delicious? Visit our on-site Pizza Shop, where every slice is baked fresh and served hot!

Our menu includes a wide variety of options, from classic Margherita and Pepperoni to our signature Jungle Feast loaded with veggies and wild toppings. Vegan and gluten-free options are also available.

Daily Specials:

  • 11:00 AM – 2:00 PM: Buy any 2 pizzas and get 1 free soft drink
  • 3:00 PM – 5:00 PM: Kids’ Mini Pizzas half price!

Opening Hours:

  • Monday – Sunday: 10:00 AM – 6:00 PM

Where will you find it?

The Pizza Shop is located near the picnic lawn, just to the left of the African Lion Exhibit.

Grab a slice and enjoy it in the shade of our picnic area while watching the animals!
