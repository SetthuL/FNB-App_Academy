MONKEY

Monkeys are primates that are known for their intelligence and playful behavior. They are found in various habitats around the world, from tropical rainforests to savannas.

Monkeys are social animals and often live in groups called troops. They communicate with each other through vocalizations, facial expressions, and body language.

Where will you find it?

The monkey exhibit is located in the tropical section of the zoo, near the bird aviary. You can observe them swinging from branches and playing with each other.

Don't miss the monkey feeding session at 10 AM daily, where you can see them interact with their keepers and enjoy their favorite fruits!
