GIRAFFE

The giraffe is the tallest living terrestrial animal and the largest ruminant. It is known for its long neck and legs, which allow it to reach high foliage.

Giraffes are social animals that live in groups called towers. They are herbivores, primarily feeding on leaves, fruits, and flowers from trees.

Where will you find it?

The giraffe exhibit is located in the African section of the zoo, near the lion enclosure. You can observe them from the viewing platform that offers a great view of their habitat.

Don't miss the giraffe feeding session at 11 AM daily, where you can get up close and personal with these gentle giants!
