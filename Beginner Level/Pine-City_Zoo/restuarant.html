RESTAURANT

Take a break and treat yourself to a delicious meal at our zoo restaurant! Whether you’re craving a quick snack or a full lunch, we’ve got something for everyone.

We offer a variety of options including burgers, salads, wraps, vegetarian meals, and kids' combos. Refreshing drinks and sweet treats are also available.

Opening Hours:

  • Monday – Sunday: 09:00 AM – 05:30 PM
  • Breakfast served until 11:00 AM
  • Lunch specials available from 12:00 PM – 03:00 PM

Where will you find it?

The restaurant is located near the central picnic area, just across from the reptile house.

Enjoy your meal with a view of the animals! Don’t forget to try our special zoo-themed desserts.
