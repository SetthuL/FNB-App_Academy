TIGER

The tiger is the largest cat species, recognizable for its pattern of dark vertical stripes on reddish-orange fur with a lighter underside.

They are known for their strength, agility, and powerful roar, which can be heard up to two miles away.

Where will you find it?

The tiger exhibit is located in the Asian section of the zoo, near the panda enclosure. You can observe them from the viewing platform that offers a great view of their habitat.

Don't miss the tiger feeding session at 3 PM daily, where you can see these magnificent creatures up close!
