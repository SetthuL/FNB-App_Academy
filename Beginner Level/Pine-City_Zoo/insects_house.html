INSECT HOUSE

Step into the fascinating miniature world of insects! The Insect House is a vibrant, interactive space where visitors can learn about some of the planet’s smallest — yet most important — creatures.

Explore live habitats filled with butterflies, beetles, stick insects, ants, spiders, and more. Watch leaf-cutter ants carry food in organized trails, or see how a tarantula hunts in a natural enclosure.

Features & Exhibits:

  • Butterfly Dome — walk through a greenhouse with free-flying butterflies
  • Glow-in-the-Dark Bugs Room
  • Interactive touchscreen displays for insect sounds and facts
  • Live feeding demonstrations on weekends

Where will you find it?

The Insect House is located next to the Reptile Pavilion and across from the picnic area.

Don't miss the daily butterfly release at 3 PM, where you can witness dozens of butterflies taking flight!
